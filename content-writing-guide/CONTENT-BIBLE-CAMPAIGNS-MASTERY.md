## PART I: PROVEN CAMPAIGN FRAMEWORKS

### The RACE Framework (Most Successful Overall)

**RACE = Reach, Act, Convert, Engage**

| **Stage** | **Objective** | **Channels** | **Metrics** | **Success Rate** |
|----------|-------------|------------|------------|-----------------|
| **Reach** | Build awareness | SEO, Social, PR | Impressions, reach | Baseline |
| **Act** | Drive interaction | Content, Email | CTR, engagement | +45% vs random |
| **Convert** | Generate sales | Landing pages, CRO | Conversion rate | +38% vs linear |
| **Engage** | Build loyalty | Email, Community | LTV, retention | +52% repeat purchase |

**Case Study: Dollar Shave Club**
- **Reach:** Viral video (26M views)
- **Act:** Social engagement (12% CTR)
- **Convert:** Landing page (25% conversion)
- **Engage:** Subscription model (90% retention)
- **Result:** $615M acquisition by Unilever

### The PESO Model (PR + Digital Integration)

**PESO = Paid, Earned, Shared, Owned**

| **Media Type** | **Control Level** | **Trust Level** | **Cost** | **Best For** |
|---------------|-----------------|----------------|----------|------------|
| **Paid** | High | Low | High | Quick reach |
| **Earned** | Low | Highest | Time | Credibility |
| **Shared** | Medium | High | Low | Engagement |
| **Owned** | Highest | Medium | Low | Long-term |

**Integrated PESO Campaign Structure:**
```
Week 1-2: Owned (Blog content creation)
Week 3-4: Shared (Social amplification)
Week 5-6: Paid (Boost top performers)
Week 7-8: Earned (PR outreach with data)
```

**Success Metrics:**
- 3.5x higher reach than single-channel
- 67% lower CAC when integrated
- 2.4x higher engagement rate

### The See-Think-Do-Care Framework (Google)

| **Stage** | **Audience** | **Content Type** | **KPI** | **Channel Priority** |
|---------|------------|---------------|---------|-------------------|
| **See** | Largest addressable | Entertainment, inspiration | Awareness | YouTube, Facebook |
| **Think** | Active consideration | Education, comparison | Engagement | Google, Blog |

```
Viral Coefficient (K) = Invites Sent × Conversion Rate

If K < 1 = Requires paid amplification
```

|----------|------------|-----------|------------|------------|

- CEO/Founder statement

- Stakeholder updates
