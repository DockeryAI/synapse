### Copyright & Fair Use

**Fair Use Factors:**
1. Purpose (commercial vs. educational)
2. Nature of work (factual vs. creative)
3. Amount used (substantial vs. minimal)
4. Market effect (harm to original)

**Content Licensing Options:**
- **Creative Commons:** Free use with attribution
- **Royalty-Free:** One-time payment
- **Rights-Managed:** Specific use license
- **Editorial Use:** News/educational only
- **Commercial Use:** Marketing allowed
